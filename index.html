Who We Are

Curating Spatial Harmony

Founded by Ana Cristina Nakano, IKE CONCEPT INTERIOR DESIGN LTDA operates at the intersection of specialized technical design and artistic intuition. Based in the vibrant heart of São Paulo, we believe that an interior is not merely a backdrop for life, but an active participant in your well-being.

Our studio moves beyond standard decoration, diving deep into the architectural bones of a property to unlock its full potential. We balance the raw, urban energy of Brazil with a "Zen-like" approach to spatial organization—inspired by the name "Ike," evoking creation and arrangement.

With years of experience in the specialized design services sector, our team handles complex renovations and delicate styling with equal precision. We craft environments that feel authentically yours, yet surprisingly new.

Whether for a high-end residence or a dynamic corporate headquarters, our mission remains the same: to create spaces that breathe.

What We Do

Tailored Design Services

Residential Sanctuaries

We transform houses and apartments into personal retreats that reflect the lifestyle of their inhabitants. Our approach involves a rigorous analysis of lighting, flow, and acoustics to ensure comfort. From the initial layout changes to the final selection of linens, we curate every square meter.

Corporate Identity Spaces

Your office is the physical embodiment of your brand's culture and values. We design commercial interiors that optimize workflow efficiency while impressing clients and retaining talent. We merge ergonomics with bold aesthetics to create productive, inspiring environments in São Paulo.

Technical Project Management

Great design requires flawless execution, which is why we offer end-to-end technical oversight. We produce detailed architectural drawings, manage contractor relationships, and oversee procurement. We ensure that the vision approved on paper is exactly what is built on site.

Our Process

How We Work

01

Immersion & Briefing

We begin by listening. We analyze your needs, the property's constraints, and your aesthetic desires. This phase culminates in a detailed project brief and mood board presentation.

02

Design & Development

This is where the magic happens. We create 3D renderings, technical floor plans, and curate material samples. We refine the design until it perfectly matches the vision.

03

Execution & Handover

We coordinate the construction, installation of millwork, and final styling. We handle the stress of logistics so you can simply walk in and enjoy your new space.
